Rain Ruihua Yang is a Graphic Designer/Architect from Vancouver. BC. Canada.
He currently attends the Rhode Island School of Design and double majors in Interior Architecture and Graphic Design.
Rain has interned for TianHe Architects Xian and is currently interning for Aedas. He focuses on functional and experimental design, he seeks to combine architecture and graphic design in his works. He is also known for his superfluous figurine collection.
